List of large earthquakes

2016 Mw-6.0 Amatrice (Italy) earthquake

  • Dynamic rupture inversion to image asperities. Work in progress.

2015 Mw-8.3 Illapel (Chile) earthquake

  • Dynamic rupture simulation based on multi-scale heterogeneous fault model (patch model). Work in progress.

2011 Mw-9.0 Tohoku (Japan) earthquake

  • Dynamic rupture simulation based on multi-scale heterogeneous fault model (patch model). Rapid kinematic inversion using elliptical model. Réf: Aochi and Ide (2011), Ide and Aochi (2013), Ulrich and Aochi (2015).

2010 Mw-7.0 Haiti earthquake

  • Dynamic rupture simulation based on segmented faults. Ref: Douilly et al. (2015).

2009-2010 Guerrero slow slip event (Mexico)

  • Investigation of frictional parameters from inverion. Ref: Maury et al. (2014).

2008 Mw-6.9 Iwate-Miyagi Nairiku earthquake

  • Dynamic rupture simulations for large amplitude of ground motions. Not achieved. Ref: Partially presented in Aochi et al. (2013).

2007 Mw-6.3 Chuetsu-Oki (Japan) earthquake

  • Dynamic rupture simulation on a conjugate fault. Ground motion simulations in 3D medium based on dynamic scenario as well as other kinematic sources for comparison. Ref. Aochi and Kato (2010), Aochi and Dupros (2012), Aochi et al. (2013), Aochi and Yoshimi (2016).

2004 Mw-6.0 Parkfield (California, US) earthquake

  • Fault slip investigation from InSAR data before and after the Parkfield earthquake. Ref: De Michele et al. (2011), Bacques et al. (2018a, 2018b).

2004 Mw-6.3 Les Saintes (Lesser Antilles) earthquake

  • Ground motion simulation. Ref: Salichon et al. (2009).

2003 Mw-6.5 Bam (Iran) earthquake

  • Fault slip inversigation from InSAR data. Ref: De Michele et al. (2008).

1999 Mw-7.4 Izmit (Turkey) earthquake

  • Dynamic rupture simlation on a geometrical complex fault system and ground motion simulations. Ref. Aochi and Madariaga (2003), Aochi et al. (2011).

1992 Mw-7.3 Landers (California, USA) earthquake

  • Dynamic rupture simulation on a geometrical complex fault system and ground motion simulations. Ref. Aochi and Fukuyama (2002), Aochi et al. (2003).

List of small-moderate earthquakes

2018/08/29 Mw1.3 Gardanne (France) earthquake and sequences

  • Forcal mechanism from local network and ground motions. Work in progress.

2016/04/25 Mw3.6 Lacq (France) earthquake and sequences

  • Forcal mechanism from regional network and near-field ground motion simulation. Ref: Aochi and Burnol (2018).

2008 LP events in Etna (Italy)

  • Forcal mechanism from local network. Ref: Trovato et al. (2016).

List of scenario earthquakes for seismic hazard study

Marmara region (Turkey)

  • Dynamic rupture scenarios and ground motion simulations with probability. Ref: Aochi and Ulrih (2015), Diao et al. (2016), Douglas and Aochi (2016), Akinci et al. (2017), Aochi et al. (2017).

Friuli region (Italy)

  • Ground motion simulations. Ref: Imperatori et al. (2010).

Moyenne Durance fault system (France)

  • Dynamic rupture scenarios with probability. Ref: Aochi et al. (2006)
