Numerical Seismology project

Etude de la génération de séismes et la propagation des ondes sismique

- Study on the earthquake generation and seismic wave propagation processes -

I have been runing my calculation at French national supercomputing center, GENCI/CINES since 2011.
  • Grant GENCI/TGCC (since 2022) and Idris (since 2023) A0YY0406700. YY = 2*(20yy-2022) + 11.
  • Grant GENCI/CINES A00Y0406700 (since 2017). Y = 2*(20yy-2017)+1.
  • Grant GENCI/CINES c201X-046700 (until 2016).

Codes (See Numerical Methods)

  • Boundary Integral Equation Method
  • Finite Difference Method
  • Boundary Domain Method
